About me

I'm a Product Designer from Brazil. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive user-friendly solutions.

I build products that not only work like a charm but also look amazing. I also have a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, which allows me to work closely with development teams. My goal? Make things super easy to use and impossible to ignore. I love adding that personal touch that makes each project feel unique and true to the brand’s soul. Whether it's a sleek app or a bold website, I make sure the message hits home in a creative way. I've teamed up with companies from all sorts of industries — and my portfolio is packed with awesome stories to tell.
